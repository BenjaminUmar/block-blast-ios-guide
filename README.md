## Troubleshooting

**App shows Untrusted Developer error:**
Go to Settings → General → VPN and Device Management → Trust your Apple ID

**Certificate expired after 7 days:**
Re-sign using AltStore or Sideloadly — takes under 2 minutes

**Installation failed:**
Ensure at least 300MB free storage and iOS 14.0 or above

**AltStore not working:**
Ensure iPhone and computer are on the same WiFi network
